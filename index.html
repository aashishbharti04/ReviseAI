📖 How to use PgluAI

Five simple steps to turn any notes into smart, exam-ready study material.

1

Pick class & subject

On the Dashboard, choose your Class/Grade, type the subject, and (optional) set your exam date for a countdown.

2

Upload your material

Drag & drop a PDF, photos of handwritten notes (auto OCR), or a .txt file. You can add several at once.

3

Process

Click Process notes. PgluAI reads everything in your browser — your files never leave your device.

4

Study with the tools

Open the Study page and use any tool below.

5

Revise & share

Download a visual PDF, listen with Voice Revision, post a Share Card, and come back when a subject is due for revision.

🛠️ What each tool does

📝 Compress Notes

Key points, definitions, formulas and important terms from long notes.

🃏 Flashcards

Auto question/answer cards — tap to flip and test recall.

❓ Quiz

Fill-in-the-blank questions with live scoring. Wrong answers become your weak topics.

🧠 Mindmap

A visual tree of the most important topics so you see the big picture.

✨ Explain Simply

Beginner, Kid or Teacher style — in English, Hindi, Urdu or Punjabi.

🔊 Voice Revision

Listens your notes aloud in your chosen language while you walk or travel.

🌙 Last-Night Mode

Emergency cheat sheet: must-know concepts, formulas only, top probable questions.

📸 Share Card

Instagram-style revision cards you can download and post.

⬇️ Download PDF

A clean revision sheet with a mindmap diagram and a top-topics chart.
